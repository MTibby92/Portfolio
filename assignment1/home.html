About Me

Matt was born and raised in Houston Texas for the first 18 years of his life before heading off to Austin, TX to attend the University of Texas. Matt graduated in 2014 with a Bachelors of Science in Biochemistry before moving to Los Angeles to work on a PhD in Organic Chemistry at UCLA. Matt quickly realized that the world of chemistry was not quite the right fit and he moved back to Austin to pursue a career in development. During college, Matt took several web development courses and found a passion for creating through code. Matt has spent the past year working in Technical Support for a software company in Austin to begin learning the ropes, and is currently furthering his development through the Coding Bootcamp at UT Austin with the goal of becoming a full stack web developer.


Matt became a Christian during his sophomore year of college, and outside of work he devotes his time to ministering to college students on the UT campus together with many of his friends and church members at Gracepoint Church, near the UT campus. Matt hopes to help plant other churches in college towns across the country, and share what he received as a college student with college students across the country.
